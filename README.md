## Overview
This project presents an **interactive business intelligence dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** and **Excel** to analyze **mobile phone sales data**.  
The dashboard provides actionable insights into **sales performance, customer behavior, and payment trends**, enabling data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Objectives
- Monitor overall **sales performance** and transaction volume  
- Analyze **brand-wise and model-wise revenue contribution**  
- Identify **city-level sales distribution and demand patterns**  
- Understand **payment method preferences**  
- Track **daily and monthly sales trends**  
- Evaluate **customer satisfaction** using ratings
